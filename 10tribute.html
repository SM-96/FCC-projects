Major General Sir Nils Olav II

Major General Sir Nils Olav is the mascot and colonel-in-chief of the Norwegian King's Guard. The name and associated ranks have been passed down through three king penguins since 1972. The current Major General Sir Nils Olav is Major General Sir Nils Olav III, who has held the name and rank since 2016.
